Animation Techniques

Name of Technique Technique Usage Reasoning Source
Fluid Emitter Used underneath Iron Man (on his boots) at the start. (was intended to be used while flying but rendering issues) Chopine states that Fluid Emitter systems are appropriate for representing particles and small objects that have a “limited lifespan” and act as an effect to the animation. Iron Man's Thrusters emit a white fire/smoke/plasma like substance, and the Gas Emitter allowed for a more concievable look to the flying Chopine, A., 2011. 3D ART ESSENTIALS The Fundamentals of 3D Modeling, Texturing and Animation, Burlington: Focal Press.
BOSS (Bifrost Ocean Simulation System) It wa used to simulate the ocean next to the city in the flyover. Autodesk states that the Bifröst Ocean Simulation System lets you create realistic ocean surfaces with waves, ripples, and wakes. This allowed for a realistic flyover of the ocean, while keeping sane render times due to its pseudo-liquid rendering system (rather than rendering every drop of the ocean). Autodesk 2018, BOSS — Bifröst Ocean Simulation System, Viewed On 3 June 2019, <https://knowledge.autodesk.com/support/maya/learn-explore/caas/CloudHelp/cloudhelp/2018/ENU/Maya-Bifrost/files/GUID-1D4CE594-25ED-4442-9C22-759E3111EFD7-htm.html>.
Camera Sequencer The camera sequencer was used to join the seperate camera shots into one dynamic scene. Wiser states that multiple virtual cameras can be used to form a dynamic and interactive scene. The maya camera sequencer can be used to generate a single camera from a series of different camera shots with their own timing. Wiser, J. 2019, Introduction to 3D Art and Animation, Viewed On 29 May 2019, <http://madwomb.com/tutorials/MayaIntro_Harvard.html>.
HumanIK Rig Used on whiplash and the more complex (Step-by step) version wa used on Iron man. Autodesk states that the HumanIK (HIK) animation middleware is a full-body inverse kinematics (IK) solver and retargeter. HumanIK tools in Maya provide a complete character keyframing environment with full body and body part keying and manipulation modes, auxiliary effectors and pivots, and pinning. HumanIK also provides a retargeting engine that lets you easily retarget animation between characters of differing size, proportions and skeletal hierarchy. The auto rig function allowed me to quickly rig the Whiplash model with one click. It wasntr perfect but it got the job done. Autodesk 2018, HumanIK, Viewed On 3 June 2019, <https://knowledge.autodesk.com/support/maya-lt/learn-explore/caas/CloudHelp/cloudhelp/2017/ENU/MayaLT/files/GUID-EDBDA3DB-4715-40EF-9ADF-412F78BFF98E-htm.html >.
